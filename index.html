Who We Are

Bridging Students & Universities

SWAG — Students With Aspiring Goals — exists to support high school student athletes as they transition to college. We provide the most well-rounded supplemental programs in the Central Valley, built on three pillars.

Founded by community leaders who walked these same streets and played on these same fields, SWAG is more than a training program. It's a roadmap for your future — from the first practice to signing day.

🏆 Athletics 📚 Academics 🎓 Recruiting
What We Offer

Programs Built to Win

Every program is designed to develop the whole athlete — on the field, in the classroom, and on the recruiting trail.

⚾

Sports Training

Position-specific coaching that develops elite fundamentals, speed, and strength for the next level.

  • Private Baseball & Softball Hitting
  • 1-on-1 Speed Training
  • 1-on-1 Strength Training
  • SWAG All Ball Futbol
  • Fermanator Bootcamp
📚

Academic Planning

We keep student athletes on track academically — because eligibility and GPA open doors that talent alone cannot.

  • P.A.S.S. Program
  • Study Hall Support
  • College Prep Guidance
  • Goal Setting & Accountability
🎓

College Recruiting

Navigate the recruiting process with coaches who've been through it — connecting you directly with university programs.

  • Recruiting Profile Building
  • Coach & Program Outreach
  • Campus Visit Coordination
  • Scholarship Guidance

The People Behind SWAG

Coaches, Educators, Leaders

Our founders walked these same streets, played these same sports, and earned degrees to come back and give the next generation what they wish they'd had.

DeVon Hilliard

Founder & Director

UC Davis student athlete turned U.S. Air Force veteran. Holds a Master's in Criminal Justice Administration. Brings discipline, structure, and real-world experience to every student he mentors.

Brett Nickelson

Academic Director

University of Washington graduate on an academic scholarship. Teacher and Associate Principal in the Merced Union High School District with a Master's in Education and Administrative Credential.

Eddie Frazier

Recruiting Director

Multi-sport college athlete who played football professionally in Berlin, Germany. Now dedicated to connecting Central Valley athletes with the college programs and opportunities they deserve.

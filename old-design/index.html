About SWAG

Who We Are

SWAG — Students With Aspiring Goals — exists to support high school student athletes as they transition to college. We provide the most well-rounded supplemental programs in the Central Valley, built on three pillars: Athletics, Academics, and Recruiting.

Founded by community leaders who walked these same streets and played on these same fields, SWAG is more than a training program. It's a roadmap for your future — from the first practice to signing day.

Programs Built to Win

Sports Training

Position-specific coaching that develops elite fundamentals, speed, and strength for the next level. Includes baseball, softball, speed training, and strength conditioning.

Academic Planning

We keep student athletes on track academically — because eligibility and GPA open doors that talent alone cannot. P.A.S.S. Program and College Prep Guidance.

College Recruiting

Navigate the recruiting process with coaches who've been through it — connecting you directly with university programs. Scholarship guidance and campus visit coordination.
